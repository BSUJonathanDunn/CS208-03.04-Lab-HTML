Kuna, Idaho -- AKA Hometown, USA!

Location

Kuna, a part of the Boise Metro Area, is 18 miles wouthwest of Idaho's Capitol: Boise.
It is also 49 miles east of the Oregon state border.

About

Population

As of the 2020 census, Kuna has a population of 24,011 people. The U.S. Census Bureau estimated that as of 2024 the population grew to 29,127 people

Name Origin

The origin of Kuna's name is currently debated, but it was named by the
Oregon Short Line Railroad in 1883 to replace the original station: Fifteen Mile Station.
Some theories suggest that Kuna means "end of the trail."
Another theory suggest that it is a Shoshone word for "green leaf, good to smoke."
Other Indigenous tribes has suggested that Kuna is a Bannock word meaning "wood."

Average Age

According to the Kuna Economic Department, the average age of Kuna's population is 30.6.
